Safety Notices

Information about how to interpret the safety notices in this guide.

Attention​

An attention notice indicates a hazard that, if not avoided, could damage or destroy the product or the users' work.

Attention:
  • Type of hazard. Result of hazard.
  • How to avoid the hazard.

Caution​

A caution notice indicates a hazard that, if not avoided, might result in minor or moderate injury.

CAUTION:
  • Type of hazard. Result of hazard.
  • How to avoid the hazard.

Warning​

A warning notice indicates a hazard that, if not avoided, could result in death or serious injury.

Warning:
  • Type of hazard. Result of hazard.
  • How to avoid the hazard.
