What's In the Box?

The contents of the NavVis VLX transportation box.

  1. NavVis VLX

  2. Hip Belt

  3. Torque wrench

  4. Batteries (4)

  5. Battery charger

  6. Charger adapters (UK / US / EU)

  7. SSD

  8. SSD adapter cables (2 cables: Type-C to C and Type-C to A. USB 3.2 Gen 2)

  9. Door stoppers (6)

  10. User Guide

  11. Hex wrench (2 x 60 mm) for exchanging the fan filter

Not shown: microfiber cloth, bag containing a screwdriver, four small head fan replacement filters, two large console fan replacement filters, an anti-glare screen protector with instructions.
